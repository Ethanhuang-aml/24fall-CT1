Final prototype presentation

This is a single-player squash game similar to Pong.

The link is here

(Recommend using Google Chrome)

Instruction

Control the paddle with your right thumb and index finger.

One point is added for catching a ball, and one point is deducted for the ball going out of the right boundary.

Improvement

1. It is no longer just the palm to control the fixed paddle, but the thumb and index finger control, so that the length of the paddle can be adjusted



2. The paddle can change the angle of the ball (still in the works)
