Final prototype presentation

This prototype is a simulation of the game "Rock, Paper, Scissors". The red cross represents scissors, the green rectangle represents paper, and the yellow circle represents rock.

The link is here

Operation method

1. Click the mouse at a random position on the canvas to randomly generate "Scissors", "Rock" or "Paper".

2. They will interact with each other as they move.

3. Click any button below, and all the graphics on the canvas will change to the graphics represented by the button.

4. Click reset and click the mouse again to randomly generate graphics again.
